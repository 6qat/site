Widgets

We are shortly going to be offering 2 juicy widgets so you can embed your Crafted.io projects into your web page or blog. This is a great way for you to show people your code examples and demos instantly.

  • Gold Seal
  • Embedded IDE

The Gold Seal displays an image only in your site. When someone clicks on it, an IDE window will open up with your project loaded and ready to run simply by pressing the Play button in the IDE.
