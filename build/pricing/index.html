    • Most people are already more than comfortable with this concept. Crafted.io supports Education and Open Source by giving the full functionality of the system at no charge. The quid pro quo is that your projects can be found, viewed and forked by anyone else.

      • Anonymous Mode

        You can use our IDE without even having to sign up. If you like what you see you can sign up later. Anonymous projects are stored using a cookie and url.

      • Missing Features

        Organizations (for billing), Teams (for assigning permissions), User Domains (for deployed Apps)

      • Acceptable Usage

        There will be an AUP coming shortly. The main purpose is to cover unusual bandwidth and storage hits for Apps that take off.
